Legend of the First Ally

Introduction

Legend of the first ally is not yet developped plan for a new TowerDeffence-Moba, Strategy with hint of RPG it brings you into lands from the family of Lord of The Rings and Dungeons & Dragons.
From the very beggining of Universe where two Dragons, the white Dragon of Creation and black Dragon of Destruction have battled constantly.
Land of humans, Elves and Dwarves have been split into good and evil, after Great Arch-Mage got cought practicing necromancy, he have been sentanced to death, right before he got killed he cast on himself powerfull dark spell that activated after his death, all the races that were at ceremony got cursed and turned into Orc's, Goblins and Trolls.

Expansion

Nobody expected that he had apprentenship that short after tryed to replikate the curse, and years later when he passed out, he awoke the curse and plaggued already cursed races into Undead's. Dark times came, Dwarves and even fearless Draws the dark elves start to escape from underworld, their home land, they were repealed by waves of skeleton's and zombies outnumbered in thousands.

Ending

In these hard times all Races got to unite, and stand together against forces of evil. This might even reqire uniting with half desised Orc's and Trolls
